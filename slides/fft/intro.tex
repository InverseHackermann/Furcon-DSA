\begin{wideitemize}
\item A Fourier Transform (FT) decomposes a signal into its fundamental frequencies
\item Typically computed with complex numbers
\item Also has discrete version (DFT)
\item Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) computes DFT in $O(n\log n)$
\item Leads to pretty ``butterfly diagrams"
\item $\operatorname{FT}(\operatorname{Convolution}(f,g))=\operatorname{PointMult}(\operatorname{FT}(f),\operatorname{FT}(g))$
